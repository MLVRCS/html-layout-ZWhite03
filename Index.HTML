Porcelain

Theses doll first appered in the Mid 1800's in Europe. These dolls popular children toys that later became collectibles. The way to find out how old these dolls are is the cracks. If the doll heavier crack it shows that the doll is very old.

Porcelain dolls are made of bisque porceliain which is a porcelain that is unglazed and matte. There also very fragile doll so make sure to hold them the right way.
